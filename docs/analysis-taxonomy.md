# Analysis Taxonomy

The first-pass analysis layer avoids investment advice. It only describes what the filing says and why a disclosure may matter.

## Fact Categories

- `business`
- `offering`
- `financial`
- `governance`
- `dilution`
- `proceeds`
- `risk`
- `related_party`
- `legal`
- `debt`
- `lockup`
- `exhibit`
- `unknown`

## Risk Categories

- `launch/space operations`
- `Starlink/connectivity`
- `AI/xAI`
- `government/regulatory`
- `financial/cash flow`
- `debt/liquidity`
- `competition`
- `governance/control`
- `dilution/share structure`
- `legal/litigation`
- `cybersecurity`
- `supply chain/manufacturing`
- `macro/geopolitical`
- `other`

## Confidence

- `high`: deterministic source or explicit section presence.
- `medium`: strong text match that still needs human review.
- `low`: weak candidate or broad table/text match.
